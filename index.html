About Me

Architecting Scalable,
Secure Cloud Enterprises

I am a Senior Cloud Solution Architect with 18+ years of enterprise IT experience, including 8+ years specializing in AWS and GCP cloud infrastructure design and delivery for Fortune 500 clients at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS).

I design, build, and implement cloud infrastructure within a well-architected framework — advising on best practices, architecting scalable solutions, and standardizing migration of on-premises workloads to cloud. I have led some of the industry’s largest datacenter consolidation programs, migrating 1,000+ VMs across 10+ datacenters to AWS and 500+ VMs to GCP.

I specialize in cloud security hardening (CIS Benchmarks, IAM least privilege, CrowdStrike, Lacework CSPM, CyberArk PAM), Disaster Recovery automation, and FinOps strategies delivering 30–40% operational cost savings.
